10 Best Food Delivery Apps in 20222

Modern technology has brought restaurants’ menu lists to your dining table. In this digital era, you don’t need to counter terrible customer service and unprofessional staff behavior in different eateries.

Online food delivery apps have done the job for you. With a few taps on your smartphone, you can get your yummy hot food from top-quality restaurants to your doorstep.

Well, Online food apps operate as food aggregators making a bridge between you and your nearby restaurants. These apps allow you to order your favorite delicious meals from a wide array of restaurants.

In addition to that, online restaurant apps reduce the effort and time to visit stores making it convenient for you to get whatever you want. As a result, a huge number of masses turned their attention toward these online food delivery apps.

Growing Market of Online Food Apps

The community of online food aggregators and their users is rapidly expanding and making massive profits.

Stats says that online food delivery apps are estimated to have a revenue of 130 million dollars in 2022. Due to social distancing and in-house restrictions in the pandemic, the graph is boosted up to its all-time highs. Mobile users spend more time browsing for online meals via food delivery apps. According to a study in 2021, the average session of a user in a food delivery app lasted about 17 minutes.

Restaurant owners are also promoting the online food delivery marketplace as it has added significant value to convincing customers and ultimately driving them to conversions.

Things to Know Before Placing an Order

Before you pick the best online food ordering app, there are some requirements you should have in mind.

Payment methods: Payment methods may vary from app to app. You should first know which method the particular app accepts. Commonly used options are credit and debit cards while some only accept Paypal.

Delivery fees:Before placing your order, get all the information and finalize the pricing including delivery fees or any extra charges. Some apps rely on your chosen restaurants while some also charge an extra fee in case you order from a non-partner restaurant.

Meeting up minimum limit:Restaurants set their minimum order limit. Sometimes apps also set their limits and they may charge extra if you don’t end up in their desired area.

User interface: Always check out the app layout or user interface while selecting an app for online food. Opt for those apps that have filters like menu price, food items, and buyers’ ratings. The app’s goal is to user-friendly interface for you.

Best Food Delivery Apps in 2022

Here’s the list of the 10 best food apps vetted by Apple and the google play app store. Dive in, choose your favorite one, and get yummy hot meals without moving an inch from your AC room.

1. DoorDash

DoorDash is the most popular food app registering a record 20 million downloads at the end of 2021. It has been the audience’s favorite Food delivery app since 2013. DoorDash is a multi-item food delivery service offering you food essentials, groceries, alcohol, snacks, and several other hot meals.

Using the DoorDash app, you can order Ready-to-eat foods from local as well as national restaurants. It also facilitates you by providing helpful instructions in finding cuisine-specific restaurants, scheduling a deal, and ordering the food.

How does it work?

DoorDash is a user-friendly app that is easy to use. After logging in you’ll have to enter a zip code or address then it will automatically load all the restaurants in your encircled area.

Upon providing you with an approximate wait time, it will show different filter results such as star ratings, restaurants that allow for pickup, vegetarian food, and menu prices to help narrow down your search.

You can track your delivery and know where your order is. DoorDash has incredible offers for its new users. They can get either free delivery or 20% off on their first order.

Ups

  • Keeps records of your favorites
  • Fast and easy payment methods
  • Simple and user-friendly interface
  • Can deliver you wine, beer, and liquor if not restricted in your city
  • Extra facilities for premium members including subsidies
  • Deliver food in multiple countries

Downs

  • Comparatively less targeted audience
  • You’ll pay restaurant taxes and tips along with app delivery charges

Seamless is the Best app for food having quick and easy delivery services. Using Seamless, you can access multiple restaurants and their whole menu list for ordering your favorites. This app provides you with Contactless deliveries with no extra delivery charges.

Seamless is basically a ranch of GorHub, both work under the same umbrella working for a year in the United States and the United Kingdom. Seamless always prioritizes customer feedback and updates its features accordingly.

Recently, it has changed its payment methods by introducing Cash and Paypal to customers’ demands. It operates in multiple countries but Newyork in the United States is one of the most prominent regions.

Being a citizen of New York it’s the best food delivery service app for you to choose. You can reach it out on any online store and download it on your smartphone.

How does it work?

When you log in to it, it will feature popular cuisines on your homepage by showing an option to search for your desired food items. Seamless has awesome offers for its subscribers like you can get 12$ off on certain orders.

Seamless sets standard delivery fees according to the restaurant you choose. This app has no commitments you can order at any time whenever you wish.

3. GrubHub

GrubHub is one of the most popular delivery apps loved by customers for ordering delicious foods from their favorite restaurants. It is known for its quick and immediate deliveries. It is a partner with almost all the popular restaurants and has an immense audience.

This app gives you access from the local eateries to top-level restaurant chains. Previously known as Eat24, GrubHub helps you in bringing whatever you want to your doorstep without moving you an inch.

It allows you to save orders and see them in the future making upcoming orders super easy and convenient.

How does it work?

Upon entering the app, GrubHub tracks your location and presents you with all the restaurants in your area.

You can pick your favorite one from a wide range of restaurants. It helps you save your location to avoid again and again entrance to the address.

GrubHub has a user-friendly interface where you can easily look up, and pick specific dishes or cuisine for your order. With its many in-app deals, this app allows you to place your order in advance.

You can enroll yourself in the list of premium members by paying a subscription fee of 9.99$ per month. GrubHub is comparatively affordable since it doesn’t charge any extra fees except that restaurant fees.

GrubHub ensures that you can sort your search results by rating, delivery time, delivery fee, and price.

  • Multiple payment options
  • A vast network of restaurant connectivity

Downs

  • Varying delivery fees between locations

4. UberEats

UberEats is a full-service food delivery app having a record 33 million downloads in America by 2021. It is a delivery service of Uber which is a riding app for traveling around the city.

It is quite popular in bringing your fav food to your home. Using UberEats, you can reach out to the menu list of popular restaurants in your surroundings.

It has cashless deliveries with many payment options like credit cards and uber credits. Its unique features like contactless delivery and real-time tracking make it worth better than other online food apps. It offers 5% off to its customers on orders above 15$.

How does it work?

UberEats doesn’t require a small cart fee in case your order doesn’t meet a certain minimum. This app is comparatively cheapest than other apps having high delivery rates depending upon restaurant pricing, sales tax, and extra fees.

Moreover, it has the lowest delivery fee of $1.49 compared to delivery fees of up to $6.49. Its promotions and discounts on UberEats can help you save some bucks in your wallet.

5. Postmates

If you’re a person with timeless cravings for your delicious foods, Postmates is here for you. It can satisfy your cravings with quick deliveries of your favorites from nearby restaurants. This app is also owned by Uber; the company delivers everything to your doorstep. Postmates aims to please its customers with its 24/7 services.

Postmates can bring you from your breakfast, lunch, dinner, yummy burger, or mouth-watering cheesy pizza to other essentials of your daily use like kitchen staples, party supplies, and medicine. Postmates is the first choice of drivers due to no time commitments.

How does it work?

Postmates has options of different categories you can select any desired space and search for your favorite item. Just pick your desired item and place the order and you’ll get it by to our home. Postmates charges delivery fees and peak factors rate inflation for free users.

On the other hand premium members enjoy relief of free deliveries without any peak factors implementation on orders above $15. New visitors to the app can get free delivery for seven days. It also has the option of a Cash-out feature to get paid directly.

The standard delivery fee of Postmates starts at $5.99 while the service fee varies by the value of the order.

  • Many locations and food options

Downs

Instacart’s delivery fees depend on the size of your ordered items and the delivery time you select.

It requires your zip code to log all its nearby partner restaurants in your surroundings. You’ll have to choose your own delivery time and location. Instacart will get your groceries or desired meal pack to your doorstep within a couple of hours or less.

It is highly rated by its users for its fast and great services. For orders, you need to add items to the cart and a shopper will deliver your items to your door. The express or premium membership provides free delivery on up to 35$ orders. Instacart has only a 4$ standard delivery fee.

Zomato is the best food delivery service app with over 00 million downloads and impressive ratings. Zomato was first started in India in 2008 and extended to the United States, United Kingdom, and Canada. This app provides you with a wide range of options from desi to fast food.

Zomato helps you provide a balanced and healthy diet with contactless delivery options. Food reviews, photos, ratings, and other details on the Zomato app can also help you choose the right items for your family while gathering at restaurants. 10. ChowNow

ChowNow is the best food delivery app for restaurant owners. It is Affordable for restaurant owners to grow their business by helping them provide tools to expand their reach.

ChowNow let the restaurants brand their online ordering system without worrying about third-party commission fees. You can get your ordered food directly through the app.

This app can be found all around the US but majorly in metropolitan areas such as New York, Chicago, LA, Seattle, Denver, Portland, Atlanta, and Dallas.

How does it work?

ChowNow instantly directs you to restaurant websites where you can browse your wanted food. The app uses a location tracking system to bring customers orders.

The main page of ChowNow shows you some search options including cuisine type or a list of restaurants in your current vicinity. It has a user-friendly interface that provides helpful information upon deciding on a meal.

ChowNow will let you know the final fee after calculating everything including meal prices, delivery fees, and service charges. Its pricing depends on the restaurant you choose and the distance.
