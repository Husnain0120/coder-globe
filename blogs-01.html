Top Mobile App Development Company in London by GoodFirms

CodersGlobe(Top Mobile App Development Company in London) is Committed To Delivering Modern, Excellent, Sustainable Solutions: Taimur Khalid

The company’s talented developers, designers, engineers, and creative artists consistently create the most elegant and functional mobile applications for clients worldwide. This article by GoodFirms aims to highlight the incredible journey of CodersGlobe and name as Top Mobile App Development Company in London, led by its CEO – Mr. Taimur Khalid. Taimur believes that transparency in business and uncompromised quality has helped CodersGlobe achieve new heights of success and never look back.

CodersGlobe was founded in 2020, with headquarters in London and other offices in Islamabad. The company is CMMI level 2 certified with years of experience developing technologically advanced softwares and mobile applications for its clients globally.

The team is determined to achieve substantial progress in the IT business by offering complete transparency and maintaining the highest product quality. An exceptional experience and knowledge with a dedicated approach helping them to deliver the best-in-class software and mobile app solutions for their clients.

Hence, CodersGlobe is considered the Top Mobile App Development Company in London by GoodFirms.

The GoodFirms team had an opportunity to communicate with CEO Taimur Khalid and learn more about the company and its values.

Paving the Way to Success

Talking about CodersGlobe and his role within the company, Mr. Taimur Khalid mentioned that CodersGlobe is a well-known mobile application development service provider. They have successfully developed more than 150 projects with 100+ clients on a global scale.

Besides, they also offer web development, UI/UX design, project management, and digital marketing. As a co-founder and CEO of CodersGlobe, Taimur’s primary responsibilities includes managing ongoing projects, client acquisition, and running ideation sessions to provide better quality services and solutions through an innovative approach.

Further, he added that while pursuing a software engineering degree, my exceptional abilities, including grasping logic, programming, and design strategies, were recognized by the IT faculty. After that, I worked for two years in the tech firm and realized it was a perfect time to launch my venture named CodersGlobe.

Dedicated In-house Team

Taimur spoke about CodersGlobe’s business model, an in-house team of developers, marketing experts, and UI designers. The company’s priority is to build a top-notch in-house team of problem solvers capable of taking any challenges and resolving them rapidly with great accountability and complete ownership to deliver the project on time and within the agreed budget.

Customer Satisfaction is an Asset

Taimur talked about customer satisfaction and its importance for the organization. We follow five pillars of values, including attitude, integrity, transparency, excellence, and professionalism; helping us to get outstanding reviews encourages CodersGlobe’s team to perform more efficiently and effectively.

Thus, customer satisfaction is one of the most critical assets for our organization; it helps us to keep a long-lasting relationship with clients and empower the team to develop excellent products.

Industries We Work With

Taimur said we work with various industries, including retail, e-Commerce education, advertising & marketing, real estate, sports, travel, hospitality, healthcare & medical, financial services, shipping, government, transportation & logistics, and entertainment & media.

Services On Demand

Taimur reveals that CodersGlobe’s most demanded services are mobile app and web development along with these services:

  1. UI/UX
  2. Quality Assurance
  3. Project management
  4. Clover App Development

Financial Affairs

Taimur mentioned that we follow a pay-per-hour pricing system when asked about the payment structure. The company accepts all standard forms of online payment modes, and although clients need to pay in any other way, we can accommodate that too.

Additionally, he said CodersGlobe’s dedicated financial team comprehends clients’ business requirements and sets the best budget, which offers value for money.

Lastly, Taimur shared his vision for the coming ten years. I desire CodersGlobe to be a more popular place as the go-to software firm for startups and well-established enterprises seeking complete development of apps, including excellent UI/UX designs with top-notch digital solutions and making them award-winning.

To know more about CEO Mr. Taimur Khalid’s views and vision for CodersGlobe’s future, read the detailed interview published on GoodFirms’ site.
